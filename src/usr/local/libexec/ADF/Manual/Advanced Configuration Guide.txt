Reflex Active Linux Firewall (RALF)

Automated Dynamic Firewall - Advanced Configuration Guide

Rev 0.1 - Draft

This guide is for advanced configuration of the Automated Dynamic Firewall (ADF) on CentOS 7.3.

The focus of this guide is the customization of regular expressions for defining the log contents to be included, and also including permanent entries.
